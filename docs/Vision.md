# Vision

## Purpose

Local-First Operations Tracker is a lightweight, configurable operations system for small businesses that need to track incoming work, customers, products, pickup dates, printable receipts, and inventory without depending on an external cloud service.

The system should be practical first: fast to use, easy to understand, and reliable during normal daily work.

## Problem

Many small businesses still manage customer work with paper notes, spreadsheets, memory, or disconnected systems. This creates avoidable problems:

- forgotten pickup dates
- missing customer contact details
- unclear job status
- duplicate or inconsistent receipt numbers
- no simple overview of tomorrow's work
- poor backup and recovery routines
- dependence on internet-based services that may fail when the connection is down

## Target users

Initial target users are small service businesses such as:

- laundry and textile services
- repair shops
- sewing and alteration services
- bicycle repair shops
- small equipment rental businesses
- local maintenance services
- small warehouses or workshops

## Product principles

1. **Local-first:** the system must work on the company's own machine and local network.
2. **Offline-capable:** internet must not be required for normal daily use.
3. **Configurable:** the workflow should adapt to different industries without code changes.
4. **Printable:** receipts and job documents must be printable and editable before printing.
5. **Recoverable:** backups and recovery paths are core features, not afterthoughts.
6. **Simple daily view:** the dashboard should show what needs attention today.

## Non-goals for MVP

The MVP does not try to be a full ERP system. The first version will not include payroll, full accounting, advanced invoicing, bank integrations, multi-company SaaS billing, or complex role management.

The MVP should prove the core workflow first.
